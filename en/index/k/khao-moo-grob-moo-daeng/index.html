Khao Moo Grob Moo Daeng

Rice with two types of pork

ข้าวหมูกรอบหมูแดง Central Thailand DE/EN
Rice Pork Pork Belly

“Khao Moo Grob Moo Daeng” ( ข้าวหมูกรอบหมูแดง ) is a classic street food dish in Thailand, where rice is served with pork belly and marinated pork. The dish is typically garnished with a hard-boiled egg and a few slices of cucumber.
