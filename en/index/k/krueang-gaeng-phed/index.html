Krueang Gaeng Phed

Red Thai Curry Paste

เครื่องแกงเผ็ด Central Thailand DE/EN
Curry Paste

Krueang Gaeng Phed" ( เครื่องแกงเผ็ด ) is the name given to curry paste for red Thai curries. It is made from dried chilli peppers, garlic, shallots, lemongrass, galangal and other spices. Other names for this paste are “Prik Gaeng Phed” ( พริกแกงเผ็ด ) or, more rarely, “Nam Prik Gaeng Phed” ( น้ำพริกแกงเผ็ด ).
