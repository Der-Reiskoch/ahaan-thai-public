Khao Pad Ga Prau

Fried rice with holy basil

ข้าวผัดกะเพรา Central Thailand DE/EN
Rice Stir Fried

“Khao Pad Ga Prau” ( ข้าวผัดกะเพรา ) is a Thai wok dish in which chopped meat is sautéed with garlic and chilies before being mixed with pre-cooked jasmine rice. Both are then stir-fried together and seasoned with a mixture of soy sauce, fish sauce and chopped holy basil leaves. Alternatively, a ready-made “Holy Basil Seasoning Paste” can also be used for seasoning. The dish can also be found under the name “Khao Kluk Pad Ga Prau” ( ข้าวคลุกผัดกะเพรา ).
