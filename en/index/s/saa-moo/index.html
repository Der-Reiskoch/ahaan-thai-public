Saa Moo

Pork with Herbs

ส้าหมู Northern Thailand DE/EN
Pork Salad

“Saa Moo” ( ส้าหมู ) is a Northern Thai dish where pork or pork offal is seasoned with Prik Laap and served together with shallots, some broth and fresh herbs. Typical herbs used include long coriander, Vietnamese coriander, mint, holy basil or lemon basil.

Name
Saa Moo
Saa (ส้า)
Salad (Northern Thailand)
Moo (หมู)
Pork
Alternative names
Saa Moo, Saa Muu, Sa Moo
Uses
