Sai Ua

Northern Thai sausage

ไส้อั่ว Northern Thailand DE/EN
Sausage

Sai Ua" ( ไส้อั่ว ) is a pork sausage prepared with a more or less spicy mixture of chilies, herbs and spices. This northern Thai sausage originally comes from the Shan State in Myanmar, but is now popular throughout Thailand. It is often produced as a sausage snail and is therefore very easy to recognize at markets.
