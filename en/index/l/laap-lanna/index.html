Laap (Lanna)

Salad With Minced Meat

ลาบ (ล้านนา) Northern Thailand DE/EN
Salad Minced Meat

“Laap” ( ลาบ ) is a salad with minced meat that originally comes from the neighboring country Laos, but has spread throughout Thailand in different variations via Isaan and Northern Thailand.

Laap from Northern Thailand, sometimes also called “Laap Lanna” ( ลาบล้านนา ), is prepared with less or no acid at all. Instead, the aromatic, dry spice mixture “Prik Laap” ( พริกลาบ ) is used, which contains among other things cumin, cloves, star anise and long pepper. Northern Thai Laap is often prepared with raw pork, offal and blood.
