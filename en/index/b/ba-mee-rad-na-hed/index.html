Ba Mee Rad Na Hed

Noodles With Mushrooms in Thick Gravy

บะหมี่ราดหน้าเห็ด Central Thailand DE/EN
Mushrooms Stir Fried Noodles Vegetarian

“Ba Mee Rad Na Hed” ( บะหมี่ราดหน้าเห็ด ) is a noodle dish with egg noodles and mushrooms. The mushrooms are prepared in a sauce made from broth, yellow bean paste, soy sauce and oyster sauce. This sauce is thickened with cornstarch and then poured over the noodles.
