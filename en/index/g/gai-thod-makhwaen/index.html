Gai Thod Makhwaen

Deep-Fried Chicken with Makhwaen

ไก่ทอดมะแขว่น Northern Thailand DE/EN
Chicken Deep Fried

“Gai Thod Makhwaen” ( ไก่ทอดมะแขว่น ) refers to deep-fried chicken pieces that are marinated with the Northern Thai spice makhwaen, salt and some fish sauce.

Name
Gai Thod Makhwaen
Gai (ไก่)
Chicken
Thod (ทอด)
deep-fry, pan-fry
Makhwaen (มะแขว่น)
Indian prickly ash
Alternative names
Gai Tod Makhwaen, Kai Thawt Makhwaen
