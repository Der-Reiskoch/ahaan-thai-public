Gaeng Khua Sapparot Hoi

Curry with Mussels and Pineapple

แกงคั่วสับปะรดหอย Southern Thailand DE/EN
Curry Seafood Ananas Mussels

“Gaeng Khua Sapparot Hoi” ( แกงคั่วสับปะรดหอย ) is a curry based on a special red curry paste, prepared with mussels and pineapple. The dish can also be found under the longer name “Gaeng Khua Sapparot Sai Hoi Malaeng Phu” ( แกงคั่วสับปะรดใส่หอยแมลงภู่ ), in which case it is prepared with blue mussels.
