Gaeng Phed Bpet

Red curry with grilled duck breast

แกงเผ็ดเป็ด Central Thailand DE/EN
Duck Curry

Gaeng Phed Bpet" ( แกงเผ็ดเป็ด ) is a curry made with a red curry paste and coconut milk. The garnish is grilled duck and various vegetables. The dish can also be found as “Gaeng Phed Bpet Yang” ( แกงเผ็ดเป็ดย่าง ) or “Gaeng Daeng Bpet” ( แกงแดงเป็ด ).
