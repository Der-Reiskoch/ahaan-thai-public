Gaeng Phed Bpet Yang Linchee

Curry with grilled duck breast and lychees

แกงเผ็ดเป็ดย่างลิ้นจี่ Central Thailand DE/EN
Duck Curry Lychee Pineapple

Gaeng Phed Bpet Yang Linchee" ( แกงเผ็ดเป็ดย่างลิ้นจี่ ) is a red curry with coconut milk, which is prepared with grilled duck, lychees and often other fruit and vegetables such as pineapple or Thai eggplants.
