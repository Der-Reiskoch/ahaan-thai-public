Goong Pad Nam Prik Pao

Fried prawns in chilli paste

กุ้งผัดน้ำพริกเผา Central Thailand DE/EN
Stir Fry Seafood Shrimp

“Goong Pad Nam Prik Pao” ( กุ้งผัดน้ำพริกเผา ) is a stir-fry with shrimp in a sweet-salty sauce made from roasted chili paste (Nam Prik Pao), fish sauce, and a little sugar. The dish is seasoned with garlic, shallots, and Thai basil, and served with jasmine rice.

Name
Goong Pad Nam Prik Pao
Goong (กุ้ง)
Shrimp, Prawns
Pad (ผัด)

Nam Prik Pao (น้ำพริกเผา)

Gung Pad Nam Prik Pao, Goong Pad Nam Phrik Pow, Goong Phat Nam Prik Pao, Goong Phat Nam Prik Pow
