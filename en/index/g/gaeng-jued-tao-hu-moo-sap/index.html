Gaeng Jued Tao Hu Moo Sap

Soup with egg tofu and minced meat

แกงจืดเต้าหู้หมูสับ Central Thailand DE/EN
Soup Minced Meat Tofu

“Gaeng Jued Tao Hu Moo Sap” ( แกงจืดเต้าหู้หมูสับ ) is a simple soup with clear broth, egg tofu, small minced meatballs and some vegetables. The soup can also be found under the name “Tom Jued Tao Hu Moo Sap” ( ต้มจืดเต้าหู้หมูสับ )

Name
Gaeng Jued Tao Hu Moo Sap
Gaeng Jued (แกงจืด)
Clear Soup
Tao Hu (เต้าหู้)
Tofu
Moo Sap (หมูสับ)
Minced pork (Ground pork)
Alternative names
Gaeng Djued Tao Hu Mhu Sap, Gaeng Jued Taohu Musab, Kaeng Jued Tao Hoo Moo Sap, Kaeng Jued Toahu Moosab, Tom Jued Tao Hu Moo Sap
