Gai Thod Gratiam Grob

Fried Chicken With Crispy Garlic

ไก่ทอดกระเทียมกรอบ Central Thailand DE/EN
Chicken Deep Fried Garlic

“Gai Thod Gratiam Grob” ( ไก่ทอดกระเทียมกรอบ ) refers to fried chicken pieces such as drumsticks or wings that are served together with crispy fried garlic. The meat is marinated before preparation in a marinade of garlic, coriander roots, pepper as well as some oil and fish sauce.
