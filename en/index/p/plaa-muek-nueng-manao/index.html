Plaa Muek Nueng Manao

Squid With Lime Sauce

ปลาหมึกนึ่งมะนาว Central Thailand DE/EN
Squid Steam Cooked

“Plaa Muek Nueng Manao” ( ปลาหมึกนึ่งมะนาว ) is steamed or sometimes boiled squid served in a sauce made from lime juice, chilis, garlic and other spices.
