Pak Khad Tao Hu Moo

Stir-Fried Cabbage with Minced Meat and Tofu

ผักกาดเต้าหู้หมู Central Thailand DE/EN
Stir Fried Minced Meat Tofu

“Pak Khad Tao Hu Moo” ( ผักกาดเต้าหู้หมู ) is a quick wok dish where fried tofu and minced meat are stir-fried together with a type of mustard green such as pak choi or Chinese cabbage.
