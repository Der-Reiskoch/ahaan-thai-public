Pho Pia Thod

Deep-fried Spring Rolls

เปาะเปี๊ยะทอด Central Thailand DE/EN
Deep Fried Fingerfood

“Pho Pia Thod” ( เปาะเปี๊ยะทอด ) is the name of the deep-fried spring rolls made from rice paper in Thailand. They are filled with a mixture of vegetables, glass noodles and often meat or prawns. They are usually served with a sweet chilli sauce or a peanut sauce. The spring rolls are often simply called “Pho Pia” ( เปาะเปี๊ยะ ). The Thai name was taken from the Chinese word for spring roll 薄饼 (Bó Bǐng).
