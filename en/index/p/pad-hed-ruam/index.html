Pad Hed Ruam

Stir-Fried Mushrooms

ผัดเห็ดรวม Central Thailand DE/EN
Mushrooms Stir Fry

“Pad Hed Ruam” ( ผัดเห็ดรวม ) is a stir-fry dish featuring various types of mushrooms. The mushrooms are fried with garlic and chilies, then deglazed with a mixture of soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce. This dish is also known as “Pad Hed” ( ผัดเห็ด ).
