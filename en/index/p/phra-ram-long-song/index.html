Phra Ram Long Song

Badender König Rama

พระรามลงสรง Central Thailand DE/EN
Chicken Pork Peanuts Water Spinach

Phra Ram Long Song" ( พระรามลงสรง ) is pork or chicken served with a peanut sauce on a bed of green vegetables such as water spinach or spinach. The name of the dish can be translated as “Bathing King Rama” (พระราม = Pra Ram), ลงสรง = to bathe). The name Pra Ram refers to the protagonist of the “Ramakian Epic”. He is depicted in many illustrations with green skin .

Name
