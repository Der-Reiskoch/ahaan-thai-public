Moo Grob

Crispy pork belly

หมูกรอบ Central Thailand DE/EN
Pork Pork Belly

Moo Grob" ( หมูกรอบ ) is the name given to crispy baked pork belly. The pork belly is often deep-fried to make it even crispier.
