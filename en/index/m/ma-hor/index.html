Ma Hor

Hearty snacks on pineapple

ม้าฮ่อ Central Thailand DE/EN
Pork Shrimp Pineapple Fingerfood

Ma Ho" ( ม้าฮ่อ ) are savoury snacks served on pineapple pieces and garnished with coriander greens and chilli rings . These appetisers consist of crushed prawns, peanuts, minced meat, saam gloe and lots of palm sugar.

The name “Ma Ho” can be translated as “Galloping Horses” and is due to the fact that this dish probably came to Thailand with mounted traders from Yunnan.

Name
Ma Hor
Ma (ม้า)
Horse
Hor (ฮ่อ)
to gallop
Alternative names
Ma Hor, Ma How, Ma Haw, Maah Hoo
Uses
Saam Gler (Saam Sahai)
