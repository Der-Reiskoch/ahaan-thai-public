Moo Kratha

Thai Hotpot and BBQ

หมูกระทะ Zentralthailand DE/EN
Hotpot Grilled

“Moo Kratha” ( หมูกระทะ ) is a type of Thai fondue in which the ingredients are not only cooked in a broth, but also grilled on the grill surface of a special Mhu Kratha grill pan. The juices from the meat flow into the broth, making it ever more flavourful . All kinds of meat, seafood, mushrooms and various vegetables are used as ingredients. Mhu Kratha is usually accompanied by various sauces for dipping.

“Moo Kratha” can also be found under the name “Nuea Yang Gauli” ( เนื้อย่างเกาหลี ) which can be translated as “Korean-style crickets” and gives an indication of the origin of this type of food.
