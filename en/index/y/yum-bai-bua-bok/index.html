Yum Bai Bua Bok

Salad With Indian Pennywort

ยำใบบัวบก Central Thailand DE/EN
Salad

“Yum Bai Bua Bok” ( ยำใบบัวบก ) is a salad with Indian pennywort. This herb is then often mixed with meat, seafood or a combination of both. The salad is dressed with a dressing made from fish sauce, lime juice, palm sugar and fresh chilis or dried chili powder.
