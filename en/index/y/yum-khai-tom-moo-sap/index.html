Yum Khai Tom Moo Sap

Salad with Eggs and Minced Meat

ยำไข่ต้มหมูสับ Central Thailand DE/EN
Salad Eggs Minced Meat

“Yam Khai Tom Moo Sap” ( ยำไข่ต้มหมูสับ ) is a Thai salad with hard-boiled eggs, minced meat and fresh herbs. The salad is dressed with lime juice, fish sauce and palm sugar. Depending on the recipe, Nam Prik Pao can also be added to the dressing.
