Yum Hed Dong

Salad with fermented mushrooms

ยำเห็ดดอง Northern Thailand DE/EN
Mushrooms Fermented Salad

“Yam Hed Dong” ( ยำเห็ดดอง ) is a Thai salad in which fermented mushrooms are mixed with fresh herbs. Everything together is then dressed with a dressing made from fish sauce, sugar and lime juice.
