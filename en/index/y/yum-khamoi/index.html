Yum Khamoi

Thief Salad with Pork, Prawns and Cucumbers

ยำขโมย Central-Thailand DE/EN
Salad Pork Prawns Seafood

“Yum Khamoi” ( ยำขโมย ) is a Thai salad that is usually prepared with pork, prawns, cucumbers, hard-boiled eggs and shallots. Unlike most other Yum salads, the dressing for Yum Khamoi uses not only the typical mixture of fish sauce, lime juice, sugar and chilis, but also the “Three Friends” - coriander root, white pepper and garlic.

The name component “Khamoi” ( ขโมย ) can be translated as “steal”, “theft”, “thief” or “robber”. So the salad could be called “Thief Salad” in English. Where this name comes from and who stole what from whom, I have not yet been able to find out.
