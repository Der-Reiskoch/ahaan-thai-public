Nam Tok Hed

Salad with Grilled Mushrooms

น้ำตกเห็ด Northeast Thailand DE/EN
Salad Mushrooms

“Nam Tok Hed” ( น้ำตกเห็ด ) is a salad made from grilled mushrooms that are dressed with lime juice, fish sauce, chilli powder and toasted rice powder. This salad is then garnished with chopped spring onions, shallots and fresh mint and other herbs. Sometimes this salad is also called “Yam Hed Nam Tok” ( ยำเห็ดน้ำตก ) or “Laap Hed Ruam” ( ลาบเห็ดรวม ).
