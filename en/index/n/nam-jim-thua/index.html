Nam Jim Thua

Sour-Spicy Dip with Peanuts

น้ำจิ้มถั่ว Northern Thailand DE/EN
Dip Peanuts

“Nam Jim Thua” ( น้ำจิ้มถั่ว ), or in its full form also “Nam Jim Thua Li Song” ( น้ำจิ้มถั่วลิสง ), refers to a Northern Thai dip made with peanuts, green chilis, lime, fish sauce, ginger and palm sugar. This dip is also known as “Nam Prik Thua Dan” ( น้ำพริกถั่วดัน ).
